Privacy Policy for AE Templates

If you need any further information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email support@aetemplates.org.

In AE Templates, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This document sets out the privacy policy for the types of personal information collected and received by AE Templates and how it is used.

Log Files

Like many other Web sites, AE Templates makes use of log files. The information includes the IP log files, browser type, ISP, date / time stamp, sign / exit pages, number of clicks to analyze trends, manage site, track user traffic around the site, and collect demographic information. IP addresses, and this information is not associated with any information to identify you.

Cookies and network settings:

AE Templates does not use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user specific information about which pages the user access or visit, and customize the content of a web page based on browser type visitors or other information when the visitor sends information via the browser.

DoubleClick Dart Cookie

. :: Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on AE Templates.
. :: Using Google's Share Cookie to serve ads to users based on visiting AE Templates and other sites on the Internet.
. :: Users may choose not to use DART cookies by visiting the ad and privacy content of the Google Network at the following URL - https://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

Some companies may advertise our cookies and web beacons on our site. Partners include advertising ......

Google Adsense

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to display ads and links that appear on AE Templates sent directly to browsers. It automatically gets an IP address when this happens. Other technologies (such as cookies, JavaScript, or network counters) may be used by third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their ads and / or to personalize the content of the ad you see.

AE Templates can not access or control these cookies used by third-party advertisers.

You should consult all the privacy policies of these third party ad servers for more detailed information about their practices as well as for instructions on how to choose not to participate in certain practices. Does not apply .AE Templates' privacy policy, we can not control the activities of other advertisers or websites.

If you want to disable cookies, you can do so through your browser options. More detailed information about cookie management can be found with specific web browsers on the websites of browsers.
